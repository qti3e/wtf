Hello

I'm Parsa Ghadimi, a programmer, and despite working in the tech industry, I mostly despise technology, mostly softwares.

There is simply too many layers of abstractions in today's modern software, and it doesn't have to be this way. I think the layers of abstractions we often add make us give up on control and efficiency of our computer systems.

I like Rust, and I love making things faster. Checkout my github to see what I have been up to.

In my day to day, I use a simple Nokia 6300 phone as it offers a much simpler user experience and does what I expect a phone to do. Make phone calls. Not everything has to be a modernized computer.

If you want to connect and have a conversation, you can find my contact information by running this brain fuck program. And if you are in NYC, I enjoy having in-person conversations about programming.
